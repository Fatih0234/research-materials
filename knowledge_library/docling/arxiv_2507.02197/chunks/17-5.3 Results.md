## 5.3 Results

Forecasting accuracy degrades over longer horizons. We observe a near-monotonic increase in MAE as the forecast horizon extends from one to six rounds, indicating that the belief-behavior consistency reduces when predicting actions Ë† sr further removed from the current game state (Fig. 1). This pattern holds across most model-archetype pairs, with the exception of Llama 3.1 8B under the M 3 and M 5 archetypes-where MAE remains flat or slightly decreases-suggesting that longer horizons could introduce accumulating uncertainty, which undermines a model's forecasting ability.
